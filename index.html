Ask Angus

Hey there, I'm the owner of AnyVan. Here are some frequent questions customers often ask me.

How does AnyVan work?

I founded AnyVan in 2009 with a mission to create the world's most efficient logistics technology — to halve the number of wasted miles driven. A lot of vans on the road are half full or entirely empty; if someone's dropping off a piano from London to Cardiff, they often drive back empty. We help our transport partners find extra work on the journeys they're already making, which also saves you a nice sum.

Does AnyVan operate near me?

We sure do. AnyVan operates across the whole of the UK, and our transport providers are based locally — no matter where you are, we'll have someone in your area. We also cover moves to and within Germany, France, Spain, Italy and Ireland.

How does AnyVan offer such low prices?

For smaller moves we're utilising journeys already happening, so we can pass the saving on to you. For larger moves we use our technology and vast local knowledge to cut inefficiencies — meaning smoother and cheaper, up to 40% less than others. We'll also price-match any like-for-like removal quote you find elsewhere.

How do I get a removal quote?

Easy — it takes less than 2 minutes. Enter your moving details on our website and you'll receive an instant price. Book online and we'll match you with an available transport provider. Or call one of our moving experts who'll assist through the whole process.
